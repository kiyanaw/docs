# Basic Controls

Here are the basic UI controls for kiy√¢naw Transcribe.

![Basic Controls](/basic-controls.png)

 1. **Transcription Settings**: Click on the transcription title to bring up your [Transcription Settings](/transcribe-features/settings)
 2. **Play Transcription**: Clicking this will play the transcription continuously from the current position. Click again to pause playback.
 3. **Zoom Waveform**: Adjust this slider to change the waveform zoom. Click the icon to reset.
 4. **Playback Speed**: Adjust this slider to change the playback speed of the audio. Click the icon to reset.
 5. **Region Controls** From left-to-right **Play Region** will play only the selected [region](/transcribe-features/regions), **Issue Button** will create an [Issue](/transcribe-features/issues) when text is selected within the editor , **Deselect Region** cancels the currently-selected region to show all Issues in the issue list, **Delete Region** will permanently delete the selected region.
 6. **Profile Icon**: Gives access to Support and Sign-out options.
 7. **Video Button**: Will restore video thumbnail when minimized.
