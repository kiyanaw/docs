# Transcription Settings

Click on the Transcription title to access the settings page:

![Transcription Settings](/transcription-settings.png)

## Settings Panel

The following settings are available:

 * [General Settings](/transcribe-features/settings#general-settings)
 * [Language Setting](/transcribe-features/settings#language-setting)
 * [Exports](/transcribe-features/settings#exports)
 * [Is Discoverable?](/transcribe-features/settings#is-discoverable)
 * [Publish Issues?](/transcribe-features/settings#publish-issues)
 * [Sharing & Collaboration](/transcribe-features/settings#sharing--collaboration)
 * [Delete Transcription](/transcribe-features/settings#delete-transcription)

 ![Settings Panel](/settings-panel.png)

### General Settings
Adjust the **Title** and add **Comments** to the Transcription, and then hit **Save Changes**.

### Language Setting
By default there will not be a Language selected for your transcription. You are free to transcribe any language you choose, however if there is a [Spell Checker](/transcribe-features/spell-check) available for your language, it will be displayed here.

#### Languages Currently Supported

 * **nêhiyawêwin** (Plains Cree Y-dialect)
 * **Northern Michif** (experimental)

Additional language support coming soon...

### Exports

* **Export Transcription**: Exports all region information to a `.txt` file. Defaults to SRT format with configurable output fields.
* **Download Source Media**

### Is Discoverable?
By default all your transcriptions are private, however if you wish for your work to contribute to the [Language Database](/language-database) you may toggle on the **Is Discoverable?** toggle and your region text will be indexed in the database and searchable for other learners.

### Publish Issues?
When enabled, [Issues](/transcribe-features/issues) will be index and available in our upcoming Issue Browser (coming soon). 

### Sharing & Collaboration
This section will allow you to add [Contributors or Viewers](/transcribe-features/sharing) to your transcription.

### Delete Transcription
Here you can delete your transcription. **Note** that all transcription, region data, invitations & media files will be deleted immediately after confirmation.
