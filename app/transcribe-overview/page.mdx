# kiyânaw Transcribe
<Callout type="info">
[kiyânaw Transcribe](https://bundle.kiyanaw.net/) is currently in Public Beta. While the application is currently stable, we may adjust current features while we incorporate your feedback.
</Callout>

[kiyânaw Transcribe](https://bundle.kiyanaw.net/) is **an online, collaborative transcription tool designed for Indigenous Languages**. It is one of a suite of tools currently being built with **language revitalization** in mind.

![kiyânaw Transcribe](/transcribe-intro.png)

## Features 
Some of the primary features of the system include:

 * [**Online Collaboration**](/transcribe-features/sharing): Invite other learners or fluent speakers to assist in the completion of your transcription.
 * [**Deep Linking**](/transcribe-features/regions#linking-to-a-region): Selecting a region or issue will provide a direct link to the related item for sharing and collaboration.
 * [**Expanding Language Support**](/transcribe-features/settings#language-setting): Additional language support can be added as transducers become available.
 * [**Issue Flagging**](/transcribe-features/issues): Mark problem words for easy identification later.
 * [**Commenting**](/transcribe-features/issues#commenting-on-an-issue): Collaborate and discuss problem spots with others.
 * [**Multiple formats**](/transcribe-features/video-controls): Current support for many video and audio formats (and more coming soon).
 * [**Access Level Control**](/transcribe-features/sharing#collaborator-levels): Private by default, share with viewers, editors, groups (coming soon) or any authenticated user.
 * [**Language Database Indexing**](/fst-support): When transducers (spell checkers) are available, index "known words" into our [Language Database](/language-database) for future discovery.

This documentation will take you through the features to help you get transcribing right away.
