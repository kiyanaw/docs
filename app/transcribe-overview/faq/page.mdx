# Frequently asked questions

#### What is transcribing?

Transcribing is simply listening to something and writing down what you hear.

#### Why would I want to transcribe something?

The physical activity of transcribing a recording of a fluent speaker is a powerful way to obtain language. Read more [here](/transcribe-overview/why-transcribe).

#### What media formats are supported?

Currently most audio and video formats are supported, from both your desktop or mobile phone.

#### Are there limitations on video?

To fascilitate faster streaming, we recommend uploading videos at 720p and roughly 1500kpbs. Videos with a higher bitrate than this may not stream properly from our systems.

#### Is my data private?

Yes. We take data privacy and security very seriously, find out more [here](/philosophy/security-privacy)

#### Where is my data stored?

Currently all transcription and multimedia data is stored on Amazon Web Services systems, primarily in the `us-east-1` (North Virginia) region.
