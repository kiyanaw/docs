# What is kiyânaw?

The kiyânaw platform is a series of online tools **designed for Indigenous Language Revitalization** by Indigenous 
language learners. 

## Our Key Principles

 * Build **one** platform capable of representing *any Indigenous Language*,
 * Focus on [intermediate learning with a goal of speech & fluency](/transcribe-overview/why-transcribe),
 * Put [security and privacy first](/security-privacy),
 * Make it free, *forever*.
